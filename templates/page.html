H2WO - Where to find your next water.

This Website helps you find the best free hangout spots near clean water, benches and toilets.

The map will show you places where you can find:

  • Drinking Water
  • Fountains
  • Benches
  • Toilets

This Website is part of the semester project "H2WO" at ZHAW School of Engineering.
