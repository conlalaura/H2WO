what do public restrooms have to offer?

Public restrooms play a critical role in creating inclusive spaces, especially when tailored to diverse needs.
From wheelchair accessibility to the presence of gender-neutral facilities, restroom attributes can significantly impact the comfort and ease of use for different individuals.
Many facilities offer free access, which reduces barriers for users, while the availability of changing tables seems to be limited. However, it's important to note that the available data is limited.

how spare is the data from OpenStreetMap?

Sparsity is a known issue in toilet data, but what about other amenities? Excluding keys like geographical data or names, how sparse is the data provided by OpenStreetMap?
Our analysis reveals that, on average, over 50% of the keys for all amenities are missing, with shelters exceeding 90% sparsity. To address this, H2W0 is excited to announce the upcoming launch of an update requester. This feature will allow users to submit missing keys, helping us enrich the data for everyone's benefit!
